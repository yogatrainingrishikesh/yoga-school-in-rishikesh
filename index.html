Our yoga teacher training course fees in Rishikesh include accommodation, three daily sattvic meals, all training sessions, course materials, Yoga Alliance certification, weekend excursions, and airport pickup. There are no hidden charges throughout your stay at our yoga school in Rishikesh.

Do I need prior yoga experience to join?

No prior experience is required for our 200 hour yoga teacher training in Rishikesh. Beginners are welcome. However, for the 300 hour advanced program, we recommend at least one year of regular practice. Our teachers adapt instructions to every level at this best yoga center in Rishikesh.
